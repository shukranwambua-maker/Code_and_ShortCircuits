About Us

Code and Short Circuits is a collective of hardware enthusiasts, embedded engineers, and tinkerers who believe that curiosity is the spark behind every great invention.

We share in-depth tutorials, project breakdowns, and hands-on guides on everything from PCB design to hardware hacking. We make sure that everything is rooted in first principles so as to remove all abstractions and allow even beginners/noobs to easily get down to work with whatever's around them.
